Lists in HTML

Unordered Lists

These lists are used for items that do not have a particular order. Types of Unordered Lists:
  • Disc
  • Circle
  • Square
  • None

Ordered Lists

These lists are used to emphasize the order of items.
Types of ordered lists:
    123
    ABC
    A.
    B.
    C.
    abc
    a.
    b.
    c.
    Roman Numerals
    I.
    II.
    III.
    Lowercase Roman
    i.
    ii.
    iii.

Nested Lists

We can place a list inside another list to create a sublist.
    I. First Period: 8:20-9:48
    II. Second Period: 9:55-11:27
    III. Block Lunch
    a. A Block: 11:27-11:57
    b. B Block: 11:57-12:27
    IV. Third Period: 12:32-2:00
    V. Fourth Period: 2:07-3:35
